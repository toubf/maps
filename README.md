## Texts used in intmaps

*   War and Peace by Leo Tolstoy: https://www.gutenberg.org/cache/epub/2600/pg2600.txt
        Letters seemed fairly normal. We concluded there weren't many Ss because the book is not written in present tense. We also theorized that the chapters being numbered with roman numerals may have contributed to the healthy counts of X, V etc
*   Journey to the West by Cheng'En Wu: https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/23962
        This very lengthy is written Classical Chinese, so it made sense that our program counted very few English characters (all of them appearing in the Gutenberg legal statements etc) 
*   20,000 Leagues Under the Sea by Jules Verne: https://www.gutenberg.org/ebooks/164 
        Also seemed normal. Large distribution of E's and T's.
